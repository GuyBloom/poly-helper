About Us

Welcome! Professor Paul the Political Parrot (or ProPaPoPa) is a tool to help break down and analyze political texts which might be dense with complicated terms or references to other bodies of work. Armed with a massive library of terms and definitions, Professor Paul takes in your input and gives you a summary of your original text in the context of the larger field of broader political theories alongside a list of the top 10 most relevant terms, each paired with a definition specific to your input.
